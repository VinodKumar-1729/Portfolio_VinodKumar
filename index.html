Blockchain Powered Certification System

Objective

To establish a secure, tamper-proof, and transparent certification system utilizing blockchain technology.

Tech Stack

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, ReactJS, web3.js, solidity, truffle, Metamask

Description

"Blockchain Powered Certification System" is a decentralized platform designed to revolutionize the storage and verification of digital certificates. By leveraging blockchain technology and IPFS (InterPlanetary File System), the system ensures the integrity, security, and transparency of certification data.

Key features and functionalities include:

  • Decentralized Storage: Certificates are stored on the IPFS network, ensuring data resilience and reducing the risk of data loss or corruption.
  • Blockchain-Based Verification: Critical certificate details, including student ID, issuer ID, and document hash values, are securely stored on a blockchain. This provides an immutable and transparent record of the certification process.
  • Role-Based Access: The system incorporates three distinct roles: Issuer: Responsible for creating and issuing certificates, entering relevant details, and storing the certificate document on IPFS.
  • Student: Receives the issued certificate and can share it with verifiers for verification.
  • Verifier: Can verify the authenticity of a certificate by inputting the certificate ID. The verifier can then access the corresponding certificate details stored on the blockchain.
  • Enhanced Security: Blockchain technology ensures the immutability and tamper-proof nature of the certification data, providing a high level of security and trust.
By utilizing blockchain and IPFS, this system offers a more reliable and efficient method for storing, managing, and verifying digital certificates, eliminating the need for intermediaries and reducing the risk of fraud or forgery.

Cattle Detection and Tracking using YOLOv9 and Deep Sort

Objective

To develop a robust and accurate computer vision system capable of real-time detection and tracking of cattle within video footage.

Tech Stack

Python, computer vision, numpy, pandas

Description

"Cattle Detection and Tracking using YOLOv9 and Deep Sort" is a computer vision project aimed at automating the monitoring and management of cattle herds. By leveraging the power of deep learning, the system provides a reliable and efficient solution for identifying and tracking individual cattle within video sequences.

Key features and functionalities include:

  • Object Detection: The system utilizes the YOLOv9 algorithm to accurately detect cattle within video frames, even in challenging conditions such as low light or occlusion.
  • Object Tracking: The Deep Sort algorithm is employed to establish and maintain unique identities for each detected cattle, enabling continuous tracking across multiple frames.
  • Real-Time Performance: The system is designed to operate in real-time, providing immediate feedback and facilitating timely interventions.
  • Accuracy and Efficiency: The combination of YOLOv9 and Deep Sort ensures high accuracy and efficiency in cattle detection and tracking, even in complex environments.
  • Scalability: The system can be easily adapted to handle various video resolutions and cattle herd sizes, making it suitable for a wide range of applications.
By automating the process of cattle detection and tracking, this project offers significant benefits to farmers and ranchers, including improved herd management, enhanced security, and increased productivity.
